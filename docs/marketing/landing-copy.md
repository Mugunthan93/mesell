## 5.2 Why MeeSell?

Three problems every Meesho seller knows — and how MeeSell fixes each.

**1. Uploading takes hours, and half get rejected.**
> Preparing a CSV for Meesho by hand can take 4–8 hours for a single SKU — and 35–60% still come back rejected. That is a full day lost on listings that never go live.

**2. Meesho keeps changing what each category needs.**
> Every product category asks for different fields, and Meesho updates them without warning. Sellers are left guessing what is required — and a missing field means another rejection.

**3. You cannot see the listing before you upload.**
> Today you only find out how your product looks after it is live. By then, fixing a mistake means editing, re-uploading, and waiting all over again.

---

## 5.3 What MeeSell does

**AI Catalog Generation**
> Describe your product in your own words. MeeSell writes the title and description and fills every field for your Meesho category — automatically. No more copy-pasting or guessing.

**Quality Gate**
> Before you upload, MeeSell checks your listing and gives it a score. It tells you exactly what would get rejected, and why — so you fix it once and upload with confidence.

**Live Preview**
> See how your product will look on Meesho before you hit upload. Catch and fix every mistake at zero cost, before it ever goes live.

## 5.6 Common questions

**Do I need to already be a Meesho seller?**
> Yes. MeeSell prepares the catalog file you upload to your own Meesho seller account. If you do not have a Meesho account yet, create one on Meesho first, then come back to MeeSell.

**Is my product data safe?**
> Yes. Your catalog data belongs to you. We never sell it or share it with anyone. You can ask us to delete your data at any time by writing to grievance@meesell.in.

**Does MeeSell work for all Meesho categories?**
> MeeSell is built for Meesho's full set of product categories and fills the specific fields each one needs. If a category changes, we update MeeSell to match.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
> Yes. The Pro plan is month to month — cancel whenever you want and you will not be charged again. Please see our Refund Policy for full details.

**Do I need a computer, or can I use my phone?**
> MeeSell works in your phone's browser. You can prepare your catalog on your phone — no computer needed.
