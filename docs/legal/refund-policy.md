## 1. Overview

We want you to feel safe trying MeeSell. Our refund posture reflects how each tier of the Service is sold:

- The **Free Forever** tier costs you nothing — there is nothing to refund.
- **Pro** and **Business** monthly subscriptions are billed month-by-month. You can cancel at any time and we will not bill you again. We do not issue partial-month refunds.
- The **Lifetime Deal (LTD)** is a one-time, scarcity-capped purchase. Because it is a long commitment, we give you a **7-day money-back guarantee** so you can change your mind.

This policy explains the details. If anything below is unclear, contact our Grievance Officer (Section 6).

---

## 2. Free Forever (₹0)

There is no payment to refund. You may use the Free Forever tier as long as you want, up to the published Free tier limits (50 SKUs per month). You may delete your account at any time per the Privacy Policy.

---

## 3. Monthly subscriptions — Pro (₹499 incl. GST / month) and Business (₹1,999 incl. GST / month)

**You may cancel at any time** from the **Account Settings** page in the Service. Once cancellation is recorded:

- You retain full access to your paid-tier features until the end of the current billing cycle.
- We will not charge you for the next cycle.
- We do **not** issue partial-month refunds — the cycle you have already paid for runs to completion.

If you believe you were charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge, a charge after you cancelled, or an incorrect amount — email our Grievance Officer (Section 6) with the subject **"Billing dispute"** within **30 days** of the charge. We will investigate, and where the error is confirmed, refund the disputed amount via the original payment method within **7 to 10 business days**.

---

## 4. Lifetime Deal (₹4,999 incl. GST, one-time, capped at 1,000 spots)

**7-day money-back guarantee.**

If, within **7 calendar days** of the moment Razorpay confirms your Lifetime Deal payment, you decide MeeSell is not for you:

1. Email our Grievance Officer (Section 6) with the subject **"LTD refund"**.
2. We will acknowledge your request within 24 to 72 hours with a tracking reference.
3. We will refund the full **₹4,999** via the original payment method within **7 to 10 business days**.
4. Your account will revert to the **Free Forever** tier.
5. Your LTD spot will return to the 1,000-spot cap, subject to availability if the cap has since closed.

The 7-day window starts at **payment confirmation**, not at first login, first catalog upload, or any other later event.

**After 7 calendar days** from payment confirmation, the Lifetime Deal is **non-refundable** and your LTD spot is permanently allocated. We have priced the LTD at roughly 10 months of Pro tier in order to make this commitment affordable and the no-refund posture fair; the 7-day window covers the realistic period in which a new buyer evaluates the Service.

## 7. Razorpay and the original payment method

All refunds are processed through Razorpay back to the **original payment method** (card, UPI, net banking, or wallet) you used for the charge. We cannot refund to a different account, card, or method, because Razorpay's settlement architecture only supports same-method refunds.

If the original card or account is no longer active, our Grievance Officer will work with you on a manual alternative — typically a bank transfer with KYC verification — within a reasonable additional time.

---

## 8. Currency, cross-border, and taxes

All prices and refunds are in **Indian Rupees (₹ / INR)**. We do not offer the Service in any other currency in version 1.0.

If you used a foreign-currency card to pay, your card issuer applied a conversion rate; we refund the INR amount we received, and your card issuer applies its own reverse conversion. The final amount credited to your card may differ slightly from the original debit due to currency fluctuation between charge date and refund date. This difference is set by your card issuer, not by us, and we cannot reimburse it.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that materially affects your refund rights, we will:
- Update the **Effective date** at the top of this policy.
- Email you (if you have a registered email address) at least **30 days before** the change takes effect.
- Show a notice in the application when you next sign in.

A change to the refund policy does not retroactively reduce refund rights that were already accrued under the previous policy — for example, if you are within the 7-day LTD refund window when a policy change is announced, you keep the old 7-day right for the duration of that window.
