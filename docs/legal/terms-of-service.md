## 3. What the Service does — and what it does not do

### 3.1 What the Service does

MeeSell helps Indian sellers prepare product catalogs that are upload-ready for the **Meesho marketplace**. Specific features in version 1.0 include:
- A category browser covering 3,772 Meesho leaf categories with their compulsory and optional fields
- AI-assisted catalog suggestions (titles, descriptions, attributes) using Google Gemini
- A QualityGate that pre-checks your catalog data against Meesho's published upload rules
- An image pre-check that validates JPEG format, RGB colour space, minimum resolution, white-background heuristic, and watermark presence
- Per-product profit and loss calculation including Meesho commission and 18% GST
- One-click Meesho-format XLSX export of your catalog

### 3.2 What the Service does not do

The Service is a **tool** that helps you build a catalog. It does not:
- Upload anything to Meesho on your behalf
- Guarantee that any catalog you produce will be approved by Meesho
- Guarantee any sales, conversions, or revenue outcomes
- Verify the accuracy of any statutory licence number (FSSAI, BIS, ISI, R-number, IS-number, CM-L number, drug licence, ISBN) you enter
- Act as your accountant, your lawyer, your customs broker, or your compliance officer
- Substitute for advice from a qualified Indian Chartered Accountant or lawyer

### 3.3 Independence from Meesho

Stellaxis and MeeSell are **independent of Meesho**. We are not owned by, affiliated with, partnered with, or endorsed by Fashnear Technologies Private Limited (the operator of Meesho), Meesho Pvt Ltd, or any group company. We use the name "Meesho" only descriptively, to identify the marketplace for which MeeSell prepares catalogs. **[LAWYER REVIEW]**

### 3.4 Free Forever tier
