The cell itself is realized (export‚Üíimage dependency exists, authorized, linter-green) ‚ÄĒ but the documented method *and* purpose both diverge from code. Likely the V1 `xlsx_with_images` format references/links images rather than ZIP-bundling raw bytes, or the byte-read is V1.5-deferred.
**Recommendation (master decision):** either (a) amend ¬ß16.B.1 8d to record `list_images` + the front-image-gate purpose as the V1 operative call (treating `get_image_bytes` ZIP-bundling as a V1.5 forward-reference), or (b) confirm with the ¬ß14 export construction whether ZIP byte-bundling is in V1 scope.
